## Skill 00: Diaphragmatic Breathing

**Subtitle:** How to transform stress breathing into diaphragmatic breathing
**Duration:** 20 minutes daily
**URL:** https://midlmeditation.com/meditation-skill-00

### Overview

Your first step is to learn to access relaxation and calm by retraining any stress-breathing patterns that may be present from your busy day. Simple changes in the way you breathe will lower stress and anxiety and prepare your breathing patterns for mindfulness of breathing in Skill 05.

### Benefits

- Desensitise mind's tendency to respond to stress and anxiety in daily life
- Make diaphragmatic breathing a natural part of practice in preparation for mindfulness of breathing

### Purpose

Retrain your breathing patterns to settle the experience of stress and anxiety in preparation for deeper states of calm and insight during mindfulness of breathing.

### Resources

- **YouTube:** https://youtu.be/VWqnvSyqG3I
- **SoundCloud:** https://soundcloud.com/user-677685629/midl-insight-meditation-skill00-20mi

### Meditation Instructions

**Step 1: Learn how to breathe with your diaphragm**

- Lie on the floor with palms below belly button
- Slowly raise and lower abdomen to engage diaphragm
- Take five slow, relaxing breaths

**Step 2: Gently slow down your breathing rate**

- Take ten more relaxing breaths by slowing movement
- Allow body to breathe naturally after 10 breaths

**Step 3: Breathe from your abdomen into your chest**

- One palm on chest, other below belly button
- Breathe from belly up into chest like a balloon
- Repeat ten times with slow, gentle breaths

**Step 4: Lie still and allow natural breathing**

- Stop controlling breathing
- Allow it to find own rhythm
- Enjoy the calming effect

### Common Obstacles

1. **Struggle and strain** - Stop and relax, avoid over-effort
2. **Light-headedness** - Stop until symptoms settle (see doctor if persists)
3. **Feeling like not enough air** - Take extra breaths if needed
4. **Breathing feels tight** - Pull diaphragm down, don't push from chest

### Insight

- **Marker:** Marker 00: Diaphragmatic Breathing
- **Hindrance:** Hindrance 00: Stress Breathing

### Daily Application

Ask: "Where am I breathing now? Is it in my chest or my belly?"

If stressed:

1. Ground yourself by squeezing hand or feeling feet
2. Place palms below belly button
3. Take five slow belly breaths
4. Wait 10 seconds and observe changes

### Progression

Ready for Skill 01 when: You experience relaxation after belly breaths and diaphragmatic breathing happens naturally during meditation.

## Skill 01: Body Relaxation

**Subtitle:** How to transform physical restlessness into body relaxation
**Duration:** 20-30 minutes
**URL:** https://midlmeditation.com/meditation-skill-01

### Overview

Increase body awareness by enjoying how nice it feels to relax your body with slow, gentle breathing. This helps alleviate physical restlessness and enhances body awareness.

### Quote

> "A meditator, having gone to the wilderness, to the shade of a tree, or a secluded place, sits down, folds their legs crosswise, holds their body erect and arouses mindfulness in the forefront of their mind." — The Buddha, MN 119

### Benefits

- Lower physical restlessness
- Access first stage of meditation - feeling comfortable in body

### Resources

- **YouTube:** https://youtu.be/mloHI_s2XUo
- **SoundCloud:** https://soundcloud.com/user-677685629/midl-insight-meditation-skill-01-transform-physical-restlessness-into-body-relaxation

### Meditation Instructions

**Step 1: Preparation**

- Sit upright with legs crossed, on stool, or chair
- Feel awareness of whole body

**Step 2: Develop mindfulness**

- Listen to flow of sounds
- Feel clothing on body
- Keep these experiences in mind

**Step 3: Relax body with slow breaths**

- Five softening belly breaths
- Five full-body breaths (belly to chest)
- Let body relax with each exhale

**Step 4: Enjoy the relaxation**

- Allow natural breathing
- Enjoy growing comfort

### Insight

- **Marker:** Marker 01: Body Relaxation
- **Hindrance:** Hindrance 01: Physical Restlessness

### Daily Application

Four things to learn:

1. Recognise physical restlessness
2. Relax physical restlessness
3. Appreciate how nice it feels to relax
4. Notice awareness increases when you relax

### Progression

Ready for Skill 02 when: You experience periods of feeling relaxed and comfortable during meditation.

## Skill 02: Mind Relaxation

**Subtitle:** How to transform mental restlessness into mind relaxation
**Duration:** 30 minutes
**URL:** https://midlmeditation.com/meditation-skill-02

### Overview

As you relax your body, a feeling of comfort will grow, making you aware of your mind's interest in past and future. You will mentally relax this interest with slow breaths out through your nose while relaxing your eyelids.

### Quote

> "The meditator understands how the arising of un-arisen restlessness comes to be, and how to abandon restlessness once it has arisen." — The Buddha, MN 10

### Benefits

- Relax mental restlessness caused by intrusive thoughts
- Help fall asleep at night

### Resources

- **YouTube:** https://youtu.be/SWwnLj9UbpY
- **SoundCloud:** https://soundcloud.com/user-677685629/midl-insight-meditation-skill-02-transform-mental-restlessness-into-mind-relaxation

### Meditation Instructions

**Step 1: Complete Skill 01**

- Sit and develop body relaxation as before

**Step 2: Relax mind with slow out-breaths**

- Take five breaths with slow exhale through nose
- Notice calming effect on mind

**Step 3: Relax eyelids**

- Allow eyelids to droop with each out-breath
- This signals mind to relax without becoming sleepy

**Step 4: Enjoy the relaxation**

- Allow natural breathing
- Enjoy comfort and ease

### Insight

- **Marker:** Marker 02: Mind Relaxation
- **Hindrance:** Hindrance 02: Mental Restlessness

**Antidote:** Be curious about what it means to relax effort in your mind. Take slow breaths out through nose while softening frontal lobes and eyelids.

### Daily Application

Ask: "Is my mind restless and inclining toward negativity?"

Four things to learn:

1. Recognise mental restlessness
2. Relax mental restlessness
3. Appreciate how nice it feels to relax mind & body
4. Notice awareness of body increases when you relax

### Progression

Ready for Skill 03 when: You can relax thoughts about past and future and feel comfortable with no desire to get up.
